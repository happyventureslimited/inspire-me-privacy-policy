Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

Welcome to Inspire Me. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the Inspire Me mobile application.

The Short Version:

Inspire Me does not collect, store, or share any personal data or information from its users. The app works completely offline and operates entirely on your device without any data transmission. Internet permission is only used to allow you to view external links like this privacy policy.

Information Collection and Use

Inspire Me is designed with your privacy as a top priority. The app:

  • Does NOT collect any personal information
  • Does NOT collect any usage data or analytics
  • Does NOT require any device permissions
  • Does NOT track your location
  • Does NOT access your contacts, photos, or any other personal files
  • Does NOT require internet connectivity to function, works completely offline

Data Storage

All content and functionality within Inspire Me operates locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties. Any preferences or settings you configure within the app are stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

Inspire Me does not integrate with or utilize any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The app is completely self-contained and does not communicate with any external services.

Children's Privacy

Inspire Me is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any information from anyone, including children, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Permissions

Inspire Me requires minimal or no permissions to function. The app does not request access to:

  • Camera or photos
  • Microphone
  • Location services
  • Contacts
  • Storage

Internet Permission: The app may request internet permission solely to display this privacy policy in your browser when requested. No other network activity occurs. The app itself works completely offline and does not transmit any data. No background data transmission occurs, and the app functions fully without an internet connection. Your data stays private and local to your device.

Security

Since Inspire Me does not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your personal information being accessed, disclosed, or compromised through the use of this app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over the app by simply using or uninstalling it as you see fit.
